What do you want to do?

Build it!

md2slides -o /tmp/slides.html main.md

Don’t you like it?

Then add some config:

md2slides -c config.js -o /tmp/slide.html main.md

You can also serve it!

With auto-reloading my friend:

md2slides -d main.md
